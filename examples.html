  • Ro-Chat

    Ro-Chat was my first ambitious product and what started me on UI-based games and application-style items. It achieves saved DMs, just like texting or using a messaging app, as well as public chatrooms, via DataStores and tables. It sustained brief popularity in December-February 2020-2021. This is personally one of my favorite projects but unfortunately is slightly inefficient and needs updating. I did most of the scripting, GamersInternational did the UI, and RobloxTitan4223 set up Trello integration and quality assurance testing.
